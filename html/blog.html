The Night Face Up

The Night Face up is a story about a man whose dreaming is attributed to life fading along with mental illness. As such we were told to use any image we liked to portray this in the form of a media poster. Choosing the cursed coin of Cortez from pirates of the Caribbean , along with an Aztec priest from Apocalypto (the madness shown in the dream takes form in an Aztec ritual) with Leonardo DiCaprio with blood markings and pain on his face to denote what transpires within the short story.
